## 1. Introduction

We are pleased to present Cobi with a proposal to develop **Coworking Space Management Software** — a comprehensive system to manage operations, bookings, contracts, payments, and customer care for a modern Coworking Space model.

### 1.1 Vision

Build a digital platform to cover the entire Coworking Space operation workflow — from space management, bookings, contracts to payments and reporting — helping Cobi optimize operational efficiency, enhance customer experience, and increase revenue.

### 1.2 Key Highlights

- **Multi-platform**: Web App (Phase 1) + Tenant Mobile App for iOS & Android (Phase 2)
- **Multi-language**: Vietnamese, English, Korean — serving international clients
- **Integrated Payments**: VNPay, MoMo, ZaloPay, bank transfer, cash
- **6 Management Roles**: Admin, Manager, Sale, Accountant, Maintenance, Investor
- **Smart Dashboards**: 6 role-based dashboards with real-time KPIs
- **Customer Portal**: Self-service booking, payment, and online contract management

## 4. Implementation Roadmap

The project is divided into **3 phases**, implemented over **10 months**:

### Phase 1: Core Operations — 🖥️ Web Version (5 months)

> **Priority**: ⭐ REQUIRED

**Phase 1 Deliverables:**

- ✅ Admin manages buildings, floors, spaces with flexible pricing
- ✅ Manager manages customers, creates bookings and contracts
- ✅ Customers self-book online, pay deposits, receive automatic confirmations
- ✅ Accountant creates invoices, records payments (including deposits)
- ✅ Maintenance records and tracks asset maintenance
- ✅ 6 Dashboards with real-time data
- ✅ VNPay, MoMo, ZaloPay payment integration
- ✅ Credit system and promotional vouchers
- ✅ Complete flow: Booking → Contract → Payment

| Stage | Timeline | Key Activities |
|-------|----------|---------------|
| Kickoff & Design | Week 1-2 | Database design, infrastructure setup, environment preparation |
| Foundation | Week 3-6 | Authentication & authorization, Building/Space management |
| Customer & Pricing | Week 7-10 | Customer management, Pricing & promotions |
| Core Business | Week 11-16 | Bookings, Contracts, Payment integration |
| Support & Dashboard | Week 17-18 | Credits, Assets, Dashboards, Customer portal |
| UAT & Go-live | Week 19-20 | Acceptance testing, bug fixes, production deployment, training |
