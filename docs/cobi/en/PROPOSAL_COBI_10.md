## 1. Introduction

We are pleased to present Cobi with a proposal to develop **Coworking Space Management Software** — a comprehensive system to manage operations, bookings, contracts, payments, and customer care for a modern Coworking Space model.

### 1.1 Vision

Build a digital platform to cover the entire Coworking Space operation workflow — from space management, bookings, contracts to payments and reporting — helping Cobi optimize operational efficiency, enhance customer experience, and increase revenue.

### 1.2 Key Highlights

- **Multi-platform**: Web App (Phase 1) + Tenant Mobile App for iOS & Android (Phase 2)
- **Multi-language**: Vietnamese, English, Korean — serving international clients
- **Integrated Payments**: VNPay, MoMo, ZaloPay, bank transfer, cash
- **6 Management Roles**: Admin, Manager, Sale, Accountant, Maintenance, Investor
- **Smart Dashboards**: 6 role-based dashboards with real-time KPIs
- **Customer Portal**: Self-service booking, payment, and online contract management
